Quinoa and Black Beans

Description

This is a very interesting dish which should only be cooked by professional chefs. Do not attempt cooking this at home or the universe could explode.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Black Beans

Steps

  1. Place the quinoa into a pot and add water
  2. Let it boil as much as you want
  3. Open a can of black beans
  4. Add them into the quinoa
  5. Remove the pot from the fire
  6. And SHOVE IT UP YOUR BUTT
